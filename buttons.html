Share Buttons

Today we're going to be making some HTML-only share buttons (view on Github):

Twitter Google Plus Facebook StumbleUpon Reddit LinkedIn Email

Share buttons are a great way to drive more traffic to a website. Unfortunately, most share buttons social networks provide are ugly and may even require loading of external scripts. Yuck. Luckily, it is possible to create share links with a single anchor tag for some of the more popular social networks. Social networks do this so users can add share links to HTML emails (email doesn't support scripts).
